Welcome to my recipe website, where I share some of the desserts I have made this year that I considered a success worth sharing! This is also serving as a way to keep recipes that I want to make again, in case I lose the "hard copy" or someone asks me for a copy of a recipe.

I find cooking to be an art form similar to drawing and music, since one must master tools and techniques, emulate masters, and practice a lot before composing one's own variation or original work. There is the additional benefit, however, that one can eat this art form, and experience it through the sense of taste and smell as well as its visual aesthetics. Like music, it is temporal: existing in space and time until it is eaten up and only the memories and the recipe remain, until -like a performance- it is made again some other time. Dessert in particular is artistic because it is not necessary for survival, but is utterly delightful and enriches life with something out of the ordinary. Dessert allows me to delight in the details, apply my perfectionism in a positive way, and thrill in tackling the challenges of new recipes.

I have been experimenting a lot lately with cheesecakes, ever since I got an Instant Pot pressure cooker that makes moist baking easy as pie. The cheesecake is a form that allows one to create many different possible flavors and toppings, simply by adding to the base with a bit of knowledge and skill. I am currently preparing to make several holiday flavor themed cheesecakes for an upcoming fundraiser for my choir. You will find the titles and descriptions on the Cheesecake page, though I have only put up the recipes for a few of them so far. Once I have actually made all of them and taken decent pictures of the result, I plan to update this section so it will be more complete. There are currently some very nice photos taken by others (with proper attribution of course) standing in until I can create my own to show what the recipe should actually look like.

In the "Other Desserts" category you will find another pressure cooker recipe, this time for that timeless French classic, Créme Brûlée. There is also a recipe for flourless chocolate cake, which I made for my last birthday. I hope to add on to this soon as well, to include recipes for some items such as Tiramisú, Chocolate Eclairs (I have to master that Choux pastry first though...), and other desserts that are neither cookies nor cheesecakes. The "Cookies and Bars"category is still a work in progress, and I hope to update it soon as well. Eventually, once I've made more cakes and pies, that category of dessert may get its own page.

I am also considering adding a page for beverages, since I have recently started making my own Chai Masala, I enjoy making mulled cider and wine for the holidays, and I'm going to try my own Egg Nog this year as well (I also plan to put some in a cheesecake...I'll let you know how that turns out).
